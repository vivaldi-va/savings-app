Do you worry about your personal finances?

So do I. This tool will allow you to plan and map your personal budget in a way that is clear and simple. It can track your budget history and plan ahead for you, saving you having to do the books!

Saving up for a special something?

By inputting your income and expenditure, we can calculate ahead to see when you will have saved enough for that certain something you have put off buying for so long.

I can help you get lean

Not every can have an immaculate budget, until now that is. By tracking your expenditure, you can analyse your expenditure and cut out the excess.

recurring finances plan for the future.

Paying the same bills every month? We can save you the tedium of having to add them every time. Input it once and set it to reoccur at an interval of your choice saving you having to find out about it at the last minute.

Reminding you of upcoming payments.

We’ll let you know of those necessary upcoming bills in advance, as well as letting you know that pay-day is coming soon with a calculation of how much surplus cash you will end up with. So you can plan ahead accordingly.

A round figure of monthly cash flow

By looking at your history and upcoming budget flow, we can give you a nice figure of exactly how much to expect in the bank. This can also be shown in a clearly understandable graph, letting you plan for your summer vacation, the next year or your retirement.
